Book OTT Platform – Simplified Product Specification 
Product Name 
BookFlix (Netflix x Letterboxd for Books) 
Version 
1.0 (MVP) 
Product Goal 
To revive reading culture by making books feel joyful, cinematic, and socially engaging, similar to 
watching content on Netflix, while using Letterboxd-style reviews, diary, history, and bucket lists to 
remove boredom from reading.

3. Review System (Letterboxd-Inspired) 
Review Logic 
• Users rate books (1–5 stars) 
• Optional short or long review 
• Mood-based tagging (Emotional, Dark, Hopeful) 
Reading Diary 
• Log reading dates 
• Personal notes per session 
• Emotional reaction 
Reading History 
• Timeline-based visual history 
• Filters by year, genre, rating 
4. Bucket List Logic 
• Users can add books to Bucket List 
• Drag & reorder priority 
• Status tracking: 
o Planned 
o Reading 
o Completed 
5. Recommendation Logic 
• Genre preference based 
• Reading history based 
• Community trends 
• Similar user behavior 
6. Engagement & Gamification 
• Daily reading streaks 
• Monthly reading challenges 
• Genre completion badges 
• Animated milestones ("You completed 5 Sci-Fi books") 
7. UI / UX Design Principles 
• Dark theme (Netflix-inspired) 
• Large book cover visuals 
• Smooth animations & micro-interactions 
• Emotion-driven design 
• Letterboxd-style minimal review pages 
8. Non-Functional Requirements (NFRs) 
• Security: Encrypted passwords, HTTPS 
• Performance: Fast image loading via CDN 
• Scalability: Cloud-ready architecture 
• Accessibility: Readable fonts, contrast support 
9. Future Enhancements 
• Audiobooks 
• AI-generated book trailers 
• Reading clubs & social rooms 
• Author live sessions 
Final Note 
This product treats books like cinema — visual, emotional, social — making reading a habit users enjoy 
returning to.
